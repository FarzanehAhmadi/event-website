About us

Welcome to World Food Events! We host events where you can taste and enjoy traditional dishes from different countries. From Persian dishes to Italian pastas, each event features delicious food, cultural insights, and a chance to explore the traditions of different cultures. Join us for a fun and tasty global adventure!

Persian Dish Event

Date: October 11, 2024

Time: 12:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Location: Dokk1, Hack Kampmanns Plads 2, 8000 Aarhus C, Denmark

Agenda

  • 12:00 PM - Opening Ceremony
  • 1:00 PM - Cooking Demonstration
  • 2:30 PM - Food Tasting
  • 4:00 PM - Persian Music and Dance

Event Description

Iran is well-known for its tasty and flavorful food, filled with fresh ingredients and spices. At this event, you'll get to enjoy traditional Persian dishes like delicious stews and kebabs. You'll also learn about the history and culture of Persian cooking. As you enjoy the food, there will be lively Persian music and dancing to add to the fun. It’s a great chance to experience the flavors and culture of Iran in a lively, festive atmosphere.
